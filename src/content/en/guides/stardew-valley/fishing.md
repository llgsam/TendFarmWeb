## Fishing Is More Than a Side Activity

Fishing in Stardew Valley is one of the most profitable skills in the early game — and one of the most satisfying once you've mastered the minigame. A skilled fisherman can earn more gold per day than a farmer in Year 1, catch rare fish needed for the Community Center, and unlock the best fishing tackle for legendary pursuits.

The minigame has a learning curve. This guide gets you through it and into the part where fishing becomes genuinely fun.

---

## How the Fishing Minigame Works

When a fish bites, a vertical bar appears. Your goal:

- **Green bar**: Controlled by you — click/tap to move it up, release to let gravity pull it down
- **Fish icon**: Moves erratically — up, down, sudden jumps
- **Progress bar** (right side): Fills when the fish is inside your green bar, drains when it's outside
- **Catch**: Progress bar reaches 100%
- **Escape**: Fish breaks free if progress bar empties completely

**The key skill**: Anticipating where the fish will move next rather than reacting. Most fish have patterns — quick bursts upward followed by slow drifts down, or short rapid oscillations. After a few catches of the same species, you'll start predicting their movement.

### What Makes Fish Harder

- **Fish difficulty rating** (shown as a colored dot): Green = easy, yellow = medium, red = hard
- **Fish size**: Larger fish move faster and more unpredictably
- **Treasure chests**: Sometimes appear during a catch — hover over them briefly to collect while keeping the fish in your bar (optional)

## Fishing Skill: What Each Level Unlocks

| Level | Benefit |
|---|---|
| 1 | Fishing unlocked |
| 2 | Fiberglass Rod available at Willy's |
| 3 | Bait crafting recipe |
| 4 | Recycling Machine recipe |
| 5 | Fisher (+25% fish value) OR Trapper (cheaper crab pots) |
| 6 | Iridium Rod available at Willy's |
| 7 | Wild Bait recipe |
| 8 | Crab Pot crafting |
| 9 | Lure Master (no bait needed) |
| 10 | Angler (+50% fish value) OR Pirate (double treasure chests) |

**Level 5 profession choice**: Fisher vs Trapper
- **Fisher** → leads to Angler at Level 10 (+50% fish value) — better for active fishing income
- **Trapper** → leads to Mariner or Lure Master — better for passive crab pot income

**Recommended path for income**: Fisher → Angler. The 50% fish value bonus at Level 10 makes legendary fish worth up to 5,000g each.

## Fishing Rods and Tackle

### Rods

| Rod | Cost | Special |
|---|---|---|
| Bamboo Pole | Free (Willy's gift) | No bait, no tackle |
| Fiberglass Rod | $1,800 (Level 2) | Supports bait |
| Iridium Rod | $7,500 (Level 6) | Supports bait AND tackle |

Switch to Fiberglass Rod immediately at Level 2. Bait reduces wait time between bites by ~50%, dramatically increasing fish caught per hour.

### Essential Tackle (Iridium Rod Only)

| Tackle | Effect | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Trap Bobber** | Fish bar drains 33% slower when fish escapes | Legendary fish, difficult species |
| **Cork Bobber** | Increases bar size slightly | Lower-skill fishing |
| **Dressed Spinner** | Faster bite rate | Any high-density spot |
| **Treasure Hunter** | More treasure chests | Income maximization |

**For legendary fish**: Always use the Trap Bobber. The slower bar drain gives you crucial extra time to recover when the fish makes a sudden move.

## The 5 Legendary Fish

Each legendary fish can only be caught once. They require high fishing skill and the right conditions.

### 1. Legend ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hardest)

- **Location**: Mountain Lake
- **Season**: Spring
- **Condition**: Rainy day
- **Skill Requirement**: Level 10 ideal (minimum 5)
- **Value**: 5,000g base (7,500g with Angler profession, Iridium quality)
- **Difficulty**: The hardest fish in the game — erratic, fast, large

**Strategy**: Level 10 fishing + Trap Bobber + Dish O' The Sea food buff. Cast as far as possible into the lake (deeper water = better fish). Be patient — it may take 10-20 attempts before the bite.

### 2. Mutant Carp ⭐⭐⭐

- **Location**: The Sewers (access via Rusty Key from Gunther's museum at 60 donations)
- **Season**: All year
- **Condition**: Any time
- **Value**: 1,000g base
- **Difficulty**: Medium — the sewer is easier than the Legend

### 3. Crimsonfish ⭐⭐⭐

- **Location**: Ocean (east pier)
- **Season**: Summer
- **Condition**: Any time, east pier cast point specifically
- **Value**: 1,500g base
- **Difficulty**: Hard — requires casting from the east pier specifically

### 4. Angler ⭐⭐⭐

- **Location**: River (north of JojaMart bridge)
- **Season**: Fall
- **Condition**: Any time
- **Value**: 900g base
- **Difficulty**: Medium-hard

### 5. Glacierfish ⭐⭐⭐

- **Location**: Forest river (south of Leah's cottage, arrowhead island)
- **Season**: Winter
- **Condition**: Any time
- **Value**: 1,000g base
- **Difficulty**: Hard — specific casting position required (tip of the island)

## Fishing Income: Is It Worth It?

Fishing in Year 1 is often more profitable than farming per hour spent. Here's why:

**Early game**: Crops take days to harvest. Fish sell immediately. A morning of fishing at the mountain lake can earn 500-1,500g in 2 hours of game time.

**Mid game**: Fish quality improves with skill. Gold and Iridium quality fish sell for 1.5-2× base value. Catfish (200g), Octopus (150g), and Pufferfish (200g) are excellent income sources.

**Late game**: Legendary fish pay massive one-time windfalls, and the Angler profession (+50% to fish value) makes every fishing trip more profitable.

**Crab Pots** (passive income): Place them in water, bait them, collect daily. They don't require the minigame and produce consistent passive income alongside your active fishing.

## Community Center Fishing Bundle

The Fishing Bundle in the Community Center requires:
- Sunfish
- Catfish
- Shad
- Sardine
- Eel (ocean, spring/fall rain, 4pm–2am)
- Pufferfish (summer ocean, 12pm–4pm)
- Tuna (summer/winter ocean)
- Red Snapper (summer/fall ocean)
- Tiger Trout (fall/winter mountain lake)
- Super Cucumber (summer/fall ocean, 6pm–2am)
- Walleye (fall river, rain)
- Woodskip (forest, any season, any time)

**Most missed fish**: Woodskip (forest area behind the Community Center, through the locked gate — unlock by repairing the bridge via the crafts room bundle), Eel (rain-only in spring/fall), and Tiger Trout (only fall/winter, mountain lake).
